Rotisserie Chicken Recipe

Season a whole chicken with salt, pepper, and herbs. Roast in the oven or on the grill until golden and cooked through. Baste with olive oil and pan juices for crispy skin. Serve with roasted vegetables or salad.

Pasta alla Gricia Recipe

Crisp guanciale in a pan, then toss with cooked pasta, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper. The cheese melts into a creamy sauce for a classic Roman dish.
